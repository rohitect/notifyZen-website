Privacy Policy

Effective Date: December 30, 2025

1. Our Philosophy

NotifyZen was built on a simple premise: your data is yours. We believe that utility should not come at the cost of privacy. Our application is designed to function as a completely autonomous local service.

2. Zero Data Collection

NotifyZen does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information, notification content, or usage data to our servers. In fact, we do not even maintain servers capable of receiving such data.

NO ANALYTICS
NO CLOUD SYNC
NO TRACKING

3. Local Processing Only

All notification interception, rule matching, and action execution happen exclusively on your device hardware. The app requires Notification Access to perform its functions, but this data never leaves your device's internal memory.

4. Permissions

The app requests the following permissions for the reasons stated:

Notification Access To listen for incoming alerts and apply your rules.
Camera/Flash Only to trigger the torch pattern actions you design.
DND Access To manage ringer modes and Do Not Disturb states.
Storage Only for local backups initiated manually by you.
